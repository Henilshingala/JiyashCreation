General Questions

What is Jiyash Creation?

+

Jiyash Creation is a handcrafted jewelry brand that specializes in creating unique, traditional, and contemporary jewelry pieces. We work with skilled artisans to bring you beautiful, high-quality jewelry that tells a story.

Where are your products made?

+

All our jewelry is handcrafted in our workshop located in Surat, India. We work directly with local artisans who have years of experience in traditional jewelry making techniques.

Do you offer custom designs?

+

Yes! We love creating custom pieces. You can contact us with your design ideas, and our team will work with you to bring your vision to life. Custom orders typically take 2-3 weeks to complete.

Product Information

How do I know what size to order?

+

We provide detailed sizing information for each product. For rings, we offer a printable ring sizer. For necklaces and bracelets, we provide measurements in the product description. If you're unsure, feel free to contact us for guidance.

Are your products hypoallergenic?

+

Most of our products pieces are hypoallergenic. However, if you have sensitive skin, we recommend testing a small piece first or choosing our nickel-free options. Contact us for specific recommendations.

Can I see more photos of a product?

+

Absolutely! We provide multiple high-quality images for each product. If you need additional angles or close-up shots, just let us know and we'll be happy to send them to you.

Orders & Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, UPI, and net banking. All payments are processed securely through our payment gateway.

How long does it take to process my order?

+

Most orders are processed and shipped within 1-2 business days. Custom orders may take longer. You'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order ships.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

+

You can cancel or modify your order within 2 hours of placing it by contacting our customer service team. After that, the order will be processed and cannot be changed.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

+

Yes! We offer special pricing for bulk orders and wholesale customers. Contact us directly for pricing and minimum order quantities.

Shipping & Delivery

+

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days within India. Express delivery takes 1-2 business days. International shipping takes 7-14 business days depending on the destination.

Do you ship internationally?

+

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Contact us for specific rates to your country.

Care & Maintenance

How do I clean my jewelry?

+

For sterling silver, use a soft cloth and mild soap. For brass and copper, use a specialized cleaner. Avoid harsh chemicals and store your jewelry in a cool, dry place when not wearing it.

How can I prevent tarnishing?

+

Store your jewelry in airtight containers or anti-tarnish bags. Avoid exposure to moisture, perfume, and chemicals. Regular cleaning and proper storage will help maintain the shine.

Can I wear my jewelry while swimming?

+

We recommend removing your jewelry before swimming, showering, or exercising. Chlorine, salt water, and sweat can damage the metal and stones over time.
